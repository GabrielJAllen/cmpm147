Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Description

I wanted to make a autumnal river scene loosely based off my inspiration image. I really like bridges so I made sure my inspiration included a bridge.

Technical

So the ground, sky, and bridge are all the same in every version and generally the bridge is in the center while the pathces of grass are on either 1/3 of the canvas. The trees are randomly placed, and are placed one by one on each side of the river, and they are drawn back to front using a variable that grows after each tree is placed. Their leaves are a simple circle with a random diameter and placed at the point of the triangle. The river is a series of points placed using perlin noise that decides the rgb values of the point, with modifiers that scale based on which color value the noise is calculating.

Reflection

The experiement went ok, I do wish I had approach this project differently, especially because I used an inspiration image only towards the latter half the project and the start was only off the top of my head. Additionally I am turning this in late and wish I gave myself proper time to work on this and polish it more.
